## LANDING PAGE REVIEW NOTES
(For existing page: https://live.zerotoheroacademy.co.in/live-fb/)

1. **Ensure headline is visible above the fold** — no scrolling needed to see the offer.
2. **CTA button must be high-contrast** — use orange or green on white/dark background.
3. **Add trust signals** near CTA — student count, testimonial snippet, or "Marathi mein" badge.
4. **Mobile-first design** — majority of Maharashtra Meta traffic is mobile.
5. **Page speed** — compress images; slow pages kill CPL on Meta traffic.
6. **Lead form option** — consider adding a Meta Instant Lead Form as an alternate funnel for lower CPL.
